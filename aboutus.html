Captain Neill Holland - President

Captain Neill Holland is a St. Pete, Florida native He completed his Sea School coursework while still at St. Pete Hight, and led University of Tampa's Dean, Doc McCann, to a Tampa Tarpon Tournament win, on flyrod, in his senior year. Hes early work as a field reporter fo rlocal legend Terry Tomalin, at the St. Pete Times Sports section, led to a degree at Columbia University. Neill's post-grad start at National Geographic magazine, led to a decade supporting the US Department of State and other federal agencies overseas. In 2015 Holland returned to St. Petersburg to chase tarpon and resume his charter operation. OA360 was inspired by issues that he and his partner, Danielle, recogbized while running the Bay - still Neill's favorite fishery after 34 countries - with fresh eyes.

Danielle Dawley Vice President

Danielle Dawley, is the Vice President of Ocean Aid 360, and a passionate champion for the environment. She has led OA360 field operations and planning fo rthe past five years, demonstrateing a great track record of community organizaing, vendor coordination, media outreach, and financial management. Danielle loves to work wih kids, to ensure that they experience comaraderie, learning, and fun while attending OA360 events. Danielle has been diving on the coral reffs of the Red Sea, the Great Barrier Reef, and the Fijian Islands and loves to be in the water. She attended University of Colorado, Boiulder as well as the University of Queensland, Brisbane and has worked as a professional photographer in NYC.
