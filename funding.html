Our Funding Approach

Ocean Aid 360 employs a diverse funding strategy to ensure the sustainability and growth of our conservation programs. As a 501(c)(3) tax-exempt organization, we leverage multiple funding streams to maximize our impact while maintaining transparent financial management.

Funding Sources

How we maintain sustainable operations through diversified support

Grants & Foundation Support

We secure competitive grants from organizations like NOAA and the National Fish and Wildlife Foundation, with our team having extensive experience in grant writing and management.

Corporate Partnerships

Strategic collaborations with businesses that share our conservation values provide financial support, in-kind donations, and volunteer resources.

Individual Donations

Support from individual donors of all levels helps fund our day-to-day operations and specific program needs.

Product Revenue

Sales from our recycled products and commercial services help support our non-profit initiatives while advancing our circular economy goals.

Financial Transparency

All resources afforded to Ocean Aid 360 are third-party audited to ensure proper management and allocation. We are committed to maximizing the impact of every dollar toward our conservation mission.
