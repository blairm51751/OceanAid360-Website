Seascraper Nets

Purpose-built tools for effective marine debris collection

The SeaScaper Net System

Designed and manufactured specifically to support clean-up efforts, the SeaScaper Net addresses the limitations of conventional tools like pool skimmer nets, which proved ineffective, inefficient, and lacking in durability for marine debris removal.

The SeaScaper Net in action during a coastal cleanup operation

Featured Product

Superior Design for Marine Conditions

Our nets are designed from the ground up for marine debris collection, with features that specifically address the challenges of cleanup operations in coastal environments.

Superior durability for marine conditions
Field-tested in emergency clean-up operations
Ergonomic design for extended use
Multiple mesh options for different debris types

Materials and Construction

  • Corrosion-resistant aluminum frame for durability in saltwater environments
  • UV-resistant, reinforced nylon mesh options for different collection needs
  • Ergonomic grips made from recycled marine plastics
  • Reinforced connection points to prevent failure during heavy loads
  • Specially designed frame shape that maximizes collection efficiency
