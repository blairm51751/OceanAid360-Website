Partners Section

Partnering for Impact

Working with Government, Public Organizations, and Industry Partners to dramatically increase the impact our organization can have

Government Partners

National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration (NOAA)

Supporting marine habitat restoration programs and providing scientific expertise for ocean conservation initiatives.

Environmental Protection Agency (EPA)

Collaborating on water quality monitoring and pollution prevention programs in coastal waters.

Florida Fish and Wildlife Conservation Commission

Joint efforts in fisheries monitoring, emergency response coordination, and marine species protection.

Public Organization Partners

University of South Florida Marine Science

Research collaboration and student internship programs supporting our scientific monitoring efforts.

Florida Aquarium

Educational outreach and marine life rehabilitation programs for rescued wildlife.

Industry Partners

Tampa Bay Marinas Association

Implementing sustainable boating practices and marine debris collection programs at member facilities.

Gulf Coast Commercial Fishing Association

Collaborative fisheries monitoring and ghost gear recovery programs with commercial fishing fleets.

EcoRecycle Solutions

Processing collected marine debris into recycled products and supporting our circular economy initiatives.

Sustainable Boats Manufacturing

Developing eco-friendly vessel designs and supporting our emergency response fleet upgrades.

Marine Tech Innovations

Providing advanced monitoring equipment and data analytics solutions for our research programs.
