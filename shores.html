Saving the Shores and Wildlife

Conservation programs focused on preserving critical coastal habitats and protecting marine species

Our Saving the Shores and Wildlife initiative combines habitat restoration, species monitoring, and community education to create comprehensive protection for our coastal ecosystems. We focus on evidence-based approaches that create measurable improvements for marine habitats and the species that depend on them.

Ghost Trap Rodeo

Our signature marine debris tournament series mobilizes recreational and commercial anglers to locate and remove derelict fishing gear while competing for prizes. These events help clear waterways of harmful debris while collecting valuable data on bycatch and habitat impacts.

Learn more about Ghost Trap Rodeo

Coastal Community Programming

We empower coastal communities through education and clear pathways to address challenges impacting adjacent marine habitat and resources. Our programs include school-based education, community science initiatives, and stakeholder engagement.

Learn more about Coastal Community Programming

Fisheries Monitoring + Evals

Our science-based monitoring programs track the health of fish populations as indicators of overall ecosystem health. We conduct statistical data management and modeling to inform resource management decisions.
