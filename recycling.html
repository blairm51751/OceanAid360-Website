Recycling the Waste

Innovative progrograms to transform marine debris into valuable resources

Our Recycling Process

Ocean Aid 360's recycling initiatives transform the marine debris we collect into valuable resources, creating a circular economy that reduces waste and supports our conservation mission. Approximately 40% of the debris we recover is successfully recycled - a significant achievement given the challenges of processing materials exposed to saltwater.

  1. Collection

    Marine debris is collected through our Ghost Trap Rodeo events and other cleanup operations

  2. Sorting & Cleaning

    Materials are cataloged, sorted by type, and cleaned of biofouling and contaminants

  3. Processing

    Recyclable materials are processed into raw forms that can be repurposed

  4. Transformation

    Processed materials are transformed into new products through our partners or in-house production

Products from Recycled Marine Debris

Our recycling efforts support the creation of our sustainable product line, with proceeds supporting our non-profit conservation work. Products include:

  • Fishing tackle and accessories made from recycled fishing line and nets
  • Ocean-themed artworks and household items from recycled plastics
  • Construction materials for marine infrastructure
  • Educational displays and demonstration materials
