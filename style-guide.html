Content Guidelines

Voice & Tone

  • Professional but approachable
  • Action-oriented and positive
  • Educational without being overly technical
  • Emphasize environmental impact and community engagement

Content Structure

  • Start with clear, concise headings
  • Use short paragraphs (3-4 sentences max)
  • Include bullet points for key information
  • Feature real impact metrics and success stories
  • End with clear call to action

Image Guidelines

  • Use high-quality, authentic photos of conservation work
  • Include proper alt text for accessibility
  • Feature diverse representation in photos with people
  • Optimize all images for web (reduce file size)
  • Use consistent aspect ratios within sections
